StreamOtter

Download non-DRM streams right in your browser — no companion app, no server, no account.

Chrome Web Store — coming soon

What it does

  • One-click detection — spots HLS, DASH, and direct MP4 media playing in the current tab and shows quality, duration, and estimated size.
  • Everything happens locally — segment download, AES-128 decryption, and merging (ffmpeg.wasm) all run inside your browser. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.
  • Proper audio — separate audio/video renditions are muxed together, so you never end up with a silent file.
  • Subtitles — downloads WebVTT subtitle tracks alongside the video.
  • Live recording (Pro) — record a live stream and save it the moment you hit stop.
  • Batch downloads & audio extraction (Pro) — queue multiple downloads, or grab audio-only as M4A.

What it deliberately does not do

YouTube is blocked at the code level and DRM-protected streams (Widevine, SAMPLE-AES) are rejected — StreamOtter is built for legitimately accessible, non-DRM media such as your own hosted videos, course platforms, and embeds you are entitled to save. Always respect each site's terms of service and copyright law.

Privacy

StreamOtter collects no data. Detection, downloading, decryption, and merging never leave your device. Read the full Privacy Policy.
